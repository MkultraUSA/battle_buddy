Capabilities

What It Does

Battle Buddy runs 24/7 on a local AMD machine, pulling public safety radio traffic and turning it into actionable intelligence.

📻

Real-Time Transcription

Listens to Travis County Law (TCSO) and Austin Fire/EMS via Broadcastify. Transcribes every transmission with faster-whisper in under 2 seconds.

🧠

AI Incident Extraction

Claude Haiku reads each log window and extracts structured incidents — type, address, severity, talkgroup — and geocodes them to the Austin metro.

🎙️

Wake Word Voice Control

"Hey Battle Buddy" activates the agent. Ask for a sitrep, ask Claude anything, then "Leave Claude" to return to monitoring. Mic mutes during playback.

🗺️

Live Incident Heatmap

All geocoded incidents plotted on an interactive Austin map. Refreshes automatically every 30 minutes. Publicly accessible at kevcloud.ddns.net/map.

📡

1,795 GATRRS Talkgroups

Full RadioReference talkgroup database loaded — APD dispatch zones, TCSO, AFD, EMS, utilities. Every transmission tagged to its talkgroup automatically.

📊

IPN Incident Feed

Polls Broadcastify's Incident Page Network for dispatcher-confirmed Travis County incidents every 30 minutes as a supplementary data source.

🔊

Spoken Sitrep

Battle Buddy synthesizes a spoken situational summary covering all incidents in the last 4 hours. Delivered via Piper TTS or on-demand via voice command.

⚙️

Always-On Services

Runs as systemd services on Ubuntu. Law stream and Fire/EMS Calls listener auto-restart. Display auto-starts on login. Zero manual intervention needed.
